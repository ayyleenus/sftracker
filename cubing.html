Cubing Tracker

Track your cubing targets across all equipment slots. Upload an equipment window screenshot, mark each piece with its target % value, and download a clean reference image.

  • ✦ Auto-detects your equipment window from any screenshot
  • ✦ Two stat line sets — pick the one for your class/server
  • ✦ Click slots or apply values to all at once
  • ✦ Download your cubing target sheet instantly
📋 Before you upload — requirements
1 Set your resolution to 1366 × 768
Options → Graphics → Resolution
2 Set UI Size to Ideal Ratio
Options → UI → UI Size
3 Take a screenshot of your Equipment tab in a visually distinct, uncluttered map — open field or sky map. Avoid towns and busy areas.
4 Hide your Special Ring slot before screenshotting — right-click the slot and select Hide.
The slot grid won't align correctly if it is visible.
