Track both star force and cubing targets in a single screenshot. Top right of each slot shows star force (0–30), bottom left shows your cubing % target. Slots exclusive to cubing or star forcing only show the relevant input.

  • ⭐ Top right — Star force (0–30)
  • 💎 Bottom left — Cubing % target
  • ✦ Cubing-only slots (Emblem, Badge) only show cubing
  • ✦ Auto-detects your equipment window from any screenshot
📋 Before you upload — requirements
1 Set your resolution to 1366 × 768
Options → Graphics → Resolution
2 Set UI Size to Ideal Ratio
Options → UI → UI Size
3 Screenshot your Equipment tab in a visually distinct, uncluttered map. Avoid towns and busy areas.
4 Hide your Special Ring slot before screenshotting — right-click the slot and select Hide.
The slot grid won't align correctly if it is visible.
